  • 01

    Step 1

    Register for an account! Create a username/password, and enter your weight. This account will allow you to track your insulin progress.

  • 02

    Step 2

    Use the food input tab to enter in your daily food. Choose from a variety of pre-set foods that have nutrition facts pre-incorporated into our database. Calculate the insulin required based on your food input, using the calculate tab. We utilized formulas from UCSF to generate accurate results, although please consult with your medical provider.

  • 03

    Step 3

    Utilize our resources tab to find out more about your type of diabetes, or use the food search tab to search our database for foods and their carbohydrate contents.

  • 04

    Step 4

    Using our history tab, you can track a log of your daily dietary habits. You'll be able to see what you've eaten, when you've eaten it, and how much of it you've eaten.
